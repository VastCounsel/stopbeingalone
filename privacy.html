Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Stop Being Alone is operated by Vast Counsel LTD, a company registered in England and Wales. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide and improve our service:

  • Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter via Beehiiv
  • Usage data via Google Analytics (pages visited, time on site, device type, approximate location by country). This data is anonymized and cannot identify you personally
  • Form responses if you choose to share your experience through our qualification forms

How we use your data

  • To send you our newsletter and updates about Stop Being Alone
  • To understand how people use our site and improve the experience
  • To shape the product based on real feedback from real people

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We use the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Beehiiv for newsletter management (Beehiiv Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics for anonymized usage statistics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify for website hosting (Netlify Privacy Policy)

Cookies

We use minimal cookies through Google Analytics to understand site usage. These are analytics cookies only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time (every email includes an unsubscribe link)

Your rights (California / US)

If you are a California resident, under the CCPA you have the right to know what data we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of any data sale. We do not sell personal data.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. Analytics data is retained for 14 months by Google Analytics. Form responses are retained for the duration of our product development. You can request deletion at any time.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure third-party providers, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we do our best to protect your data.

Children

Stop Being Alone is not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
